What's the difference between a merge and a rebase?

Merge is used to combine two branches and integrate updated changes into the local repo to the working directory. Rebase updates the start of your branch to a later
commit that happpened after you branched out

Rebase: If you want to update the start of your branch to a later commit that happend after you branched out

Merge: integrate those updated changes in the local repo to the working directory


What is a remote repository?

Remote repos are versions of your project that are hosted on the network. You work on your local repo, and you update your work by pushing to the local repo.
